My Top 10 Virtues

Written 2007, translated 2013.

Values are subjective; different people have different ideas of what is important and good in this world. Below, I've listed out my own version of the "10 commandments". I wrote it for a high-school English class assignment. (Thanks Mr. Day.)

人的價值是主觀的，對這個世界裡的好壞善惡，不同的人持不同的觀點。 以下我列出了我自己的“十誡”。這是我上高中英文課寫的。

  1. Intelligence: Be thoughtful and reasonable.
  2. Kindness: Be compassionate and good-willed.
  3. Peacefulness: Be calm and tranquil.
  4. Autonomy: Be independent and resourceful.
  5. Adaptability: Be flexible and not stubborn.
  6. Reliability: Be dependable and trustworthy.
  7. Industriousness: Do not be lazy, do not waste time.
  8. Restraint: Have self-discipline and self-control.
  9. Contendedness: Be satisfied and not greedy.
  10. Lightheartedness: Be cheerful and good-humored.
  1. 智慧：深思熟慮、知足多謀
  2. 仁慈：同情他人、有善意
  3. 平和：性情温和、平易近人
  4. 自主：知足多謀、獨立
  5. 靈活：隨遇而安、善於變通
  6. 可靠：值得信賴
  7. 勤奮：不懶惰、今日事今日畢
  8. 自律：自我控制、有自制力
  9. 知足：不貪婪、知足常樂
  10. 開朗：無憂無慮、輕鬆愉快
